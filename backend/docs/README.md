### Remote Work Compliance & Task Tracker


## Key Features
• Digital attendance with geo-fencing and IP-based authentication for secure remote check-ins.
• Task management system with deadlines, progress tracking, and priority suggestions.
• AI-powered productivity insights, including efficiency scoring and productivity trend analysis.
• Automated labor law compliance monitoring with alerts for overtime and irregular work patterns.
• Payroll automation with overtime calculation and performance-based bonus suggestions.
• Employee wellness monitoring with mood tracking, burnout detection, and break reminders.
• Smart goal and KPI tracking aligned with tasks and projects.
• Automated performance review generation using employee productivity and engagement data.
• Smart notifications and alerts for overdue tasks, missing check-ins, and workload imbalances.
• Learning and upskilling integration with AI-recommended training programs.
• Advanced analytics dashboards for managers and HR teams.
• Secure audit logs and digital signatures for compliance verification.


## Web Application
Functional Requirements
• User authentication and role-based access control (Admin, Manager, Employee).
• Employee attendance tracking with location verification and time logging.
• Task creation, assignment, progress tracking, and deadline management.
• Dashboard displaying employee productivity, task status, and team performance.
• Compliance monitoring with alerts for overtime or policy violations.
• Payroll management, including salary calculation, overtime, and bonuses.
• Goal and KPI management with progress tracking.
• Performance review generation based on collected employee data.
• Reporting and analytics with customizable dashboards.
• Integration with training modules for employee skill development.
• Notification system for tasks, attendance reminders, and alerts.
Non-Functional Requirements
• High system availability and reliability for remote workforce operations.
• Secure authentication and data encryption to protect employee data.
• Scalability to support growing numbers of users and organizations.
• Fast response time for dashboards and analytics queries.
• Cross-browser compatibility (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari).
• Maintainability with modular architecture and clear API design.
• Data privacy compliance with applicable labor and data protection regulations.
• Auditability with time-stamped logs for all critical system actions.




## Mobile Application
Functional Requirements
• Secure login and authentication for employees.
• Mobile attendance check-in and check-out with location verification.
• Task viewing, updates, and progress tracking.
• Real-time notifications for tasks, deadlines, and reminders.
• Mood and wellness check-ins for employee well-being tracking.
• Access to personal productivity insights and KPI progress.
• Quick communication or social interaction through a virtual team space.
• Access to recommended training modules and learning resources.
Non-Functional Requirements
• Lightweight and responsive design for smooth performance on mobile devices.
• Offline capability for task viewing and attendance logging with later synchronization.
• Secure data transmission using encrypted communication.
• Compatibility with both Android and iOS devices.
• Low battery and data consumption during continuous use.
• High usability with an intuitive interface for quick employee interactions.
